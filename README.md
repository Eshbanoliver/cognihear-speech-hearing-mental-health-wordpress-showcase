# CogniHear - Speech, Hearing & Mental Health Clinic Website



Custom WordPress website developed for CogniHear, a leading integrated clinic offering speech therapy, hearing care solutions, and mental health services in Udaipur, Rajasthan (2025 client project).

## Project Overview
- **Client Industry**: Healthcare & Wellness (Speech Therapy, Audiology/Hearing Aids, Mental Health Counseling)
- **My Role**: Full custom WordPress development, theme customization, service layouts, performance optimization
- **Timeline**: 4-6 weeks
- **Team Size**: Solo developer
- **Live Website**: [https://cognihear.com/](https://cognihear.com/)

## Key Features Implemented
- Hero section with empowering taglines ("Hear Better. Speak Freely. Live Mentally Strong.", "Where Sound Meets Peace of Mind") and focus on integrated care
- Services showcase: Free Hearing Tests, Advanced Hearing Aids (battery-operated, rechargeable, Bluetooth-enabled), Speech Therapy, Mental Health Counseling (anxiety, stress, relationships, couple therapy, IQ/career assessments)
- About section highlighting 10+ years experience, certified audiologists/therapists, one-stop clinic, trusted reputation, and commitment to clear hearing, confident speech, and mental strength
- Stats counters: 1000+ Hearing Tests Conducted, 600+ Hearing Aids Fitted, 500+ Mental Health Sessions, 98% Successful Clients
- Testimonials section with real client feedback on hearing aid quality, speech improvement, and counseling effectiveness
- Doctor profiles ("The Faces Behind Your Care") and FAQ covering services, free tests, and clinic details
- Responsive, calming design optimized for health/wellness visuals (mobile-first)
- SEO-friendly for local Udaipur clinic searches (hearing aids, speech therapy, mental health)

## Technical Stack
- WordPress v6.7+
- PHP 8.2+
- Elementor Pro (or similar builder) + custom CSS for clean, trustworthy health styling and service cards
- JavaScript for stats counters, testimonials carousel, smooth interactions
- Plugins: Elementor, contact/booking forms (WPForms or similar), Yoast SEO, image optimization/cache
- Performance: WebP images, lazy loading for clinic/equipment photos
